The School for Good and Evil

The School for Good and Evil is a 5 book long series which was completed in 2020. The story is about to girls named Sophie and Agatha, who are kidnapped to go to the School for Good and Evil. Sophie, with her undeniable beauty seems perfect for the School for Good, while Agatha with her with goth-like attitude, seems a natural fit for the School for Evil. However, when the two girls are swept off to the school, their fates are reversed, with Agatha in the School for Good and Sophie in the School for Evil.

Review

The School for Good and Evil is a wonderfully written middle-grade novel, filled with adventure and unpredictability. It takes all of the fairytale stereotypes and throws them in the garbage. The School for Good and Evil was real, beautiful and enchanting, and each character was unique and built amazingly. I would recommend this book to anyone looking for a unique spin on classic fairytales and how they're viewed.
