The School for Good and Evil

The School for Good and Evil is a 5 book long series which was complted in 2020. The story is about to girls named Sophie and Agatha, who will be kidnapped to go to the School for Good and Evil. Sophie, with her undeniable beauty seems perfect for the School for Good, while Agatha with her with goth-like attitude, seems a natural fit for the School for Evil. However, when the two girls are swept off to the school, their fates are reversed, with Agatha in the School for Good and Sophie in the School for Evil.
