Keeper of the Lost Cities

Keeper of the Lost Cities is an 8 book long series which is still being written about a twelve year old high school senior named Sophie Foster, who, also happens to be a telepath. No one know this, but that changes when a mysterious boy named Fitz Vacker who can also read minds approaches her. She finds out that there is a place where she belongs, but she must leave the world she knows behind.

Review

Keeper of the Lost Cities is one of my favourite middle grade series of all time. The writing style was amazing and the plot is sure to captivate you. The characters are each fully developed and completely lovable. The pages are full of intrigue and twists and mystery that will leave you unable to stop thinking about it. Perfect for fans of Harry Potter, all readers are sure to love Keeper of the Lost Cities.

Excerpt

Blurry, fractured memories swam through Sophie’s mind, but she couldn’t piece them together. She tried opening her eyes and found only darkness. Something rough pressed against her wrists and ankles, refusing to let her move.

A wave of cold rushed through her as the horrifying realization dawned.

She was a hostage.
