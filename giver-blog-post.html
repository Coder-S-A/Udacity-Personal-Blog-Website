The Giver is a story of a brilliant boy named Jonas who lives in a society that is being controlled by the rules and tradition of The Elders. Through his wisdom and mannerism, Jonas is selected as the Receiver of Memory, who is know as the giver. This post distinguishes him from others and gives him authority. He follows the rules and receives wisdom in the form of memories, but what he sees upsets him. This, is the story of the Giver.
