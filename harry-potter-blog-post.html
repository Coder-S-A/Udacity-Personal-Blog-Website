Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a 7 book long series that has been made into 8 movies. It is about an 11 year old boy named Harry who is a wizard. Throughout this story Harry finds truths about himslef and others which he could never have imagined to be true. With betrayals and friendships, this is the story of the adventures which Harry goes on to help save the wizarding world from destruction. It is a classic fantasy read from three-headed-dogs to evil sorcerers.

Review

Harry Potter is a wonderful novel and the entire series was filled with interesting twists and turns. I have read and reread the Harry Potter series many, many times and it never gets old. It is filled with funny, courageous, and endearing characters that you can't help but love. The world of Harry Potter is richly layered and wildly imaginative. For all these reasons, it's no surprise that Harry Potter is such a beloved book, by not only by kids, but also by adults.
