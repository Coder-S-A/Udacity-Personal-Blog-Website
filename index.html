Fantasy

Keeper of the Lost Cities

Keeper of the lost Cities is an 8 book long series which is still being written. It's about a twelve year old high school senior named Sophie Foster, who, also happens to be a telepath. One day Sophie finds out that she is an elf and goes to a new world filled with magic and adventure

Fantasy

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a 7 book long series that has been made into 8 movies. It is about an 11 year old boy named Harry who finds out that he is a wizard and will be going to Hogwarts, a school for wizards and witches.

Fantasy

The School for Good and Evil

The School for Good and Evil is a 6 book long series. It is about two girls named Sophie and Agatha. Sophie seems like a perfect fit for the School for Good and Agatha for the School for Evil. But one day, their fates are reversed and they begin to learn who they really are.

Science Fiction

Red Rising

Red Rising is a 5 book long series that is still being written. It is about Darrow, a boy who is part of the lowest class of society, the reds. One day, Darrow discovers that humanity reached the surface generations ago, and they left the reds as slaves. Longing for justice, he risks everything to infiltrate the Gold's society.

Science Fiction

The Martian

The Martian is a stand-alone novel which has been made into a movie. It is about Mark Watney, an astronaut, engineer and botanist who ends up stranded on Mars. This is the story of how he uses his skills and finds a way to survive.

Science Fiction

The Giver

The Giver is a stand-alone novel about a boy named Jonas who lives in a colourless world that is controlled by The Elders. One day, Jonas is selected to be the next Receiver of Memory, or, Giver. It's a great honour to recieve this title, but it means that he will learn the truth about his world.
