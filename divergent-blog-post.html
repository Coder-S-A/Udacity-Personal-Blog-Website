Divergent

In a futuristic Chicago, society is divided into 5 factions. Abnegation the selfless, Dauntless the brave, Erudite the intelligent, Candor the honest, and Amity the peaceful. Tris learns she's Divergent, meaning she's a mix of factions, and won't fit in. When she discovers a plot to destroy Divergents, Tris and a boy called Four must find out what makes Divergents dangerous before it's too late. This is the story of the Divergent.
