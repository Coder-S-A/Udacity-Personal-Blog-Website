Red Rising

Red Rising is about Darrow, a boy who is part of the lowest class of society, the reds. He works constantly, believing that he's making Mars habitable. Soon, Darrow discovers that humanity reached the surface generations ago, and they left the reds as slaves. Longing for justice, and driven by the memory of lost love he risks everything to infiltrate the Gold's society.

Review

Red Rising is one of the best science fiction books there are. Often compared to the Hunger Games, it is a wonderfully crafted novel, set in a dystopian future. Red Rising is perfect for all ages, and while it is about a 16 year old boy, it does not follow any of the normal young adult conventions. The characters are all unique and beautifully written. There is never a dull moment, and readers can expect to be completely unable to put the book down.
