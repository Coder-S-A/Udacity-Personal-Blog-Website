The Martian

The Martian is the story of an astronaut and engineer named Mark Watney, who became one of the first people to walk on Mars and 6 days later became the first person to be stranded on Mars. As Mark fights to survive, he must find creative ways to get sustenance and communicate with the people on Earth. Will his resourcefulness be enough to overcome the impossible odds against him?

Review

Once you pick up The Martian, you won't be able to put it down until it's complete. Filled with suspense you never know what will happne next. The Martian is meticulously researched, briskly paced and filled with unexpexted humour. Perfect for all readers, the Martian is brilliantly written and is a perfectly realistic science fiction novel.
